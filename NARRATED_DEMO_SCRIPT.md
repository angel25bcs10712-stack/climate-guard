# 🎙️ ClimateGuard — Official 3-Minute Demo Script

> **Recording Tip:** Keep your screen recording software ready. Practice this flow once before recording. Speak clearly and confidently!

---

## 🕒 [0:00 - 0:30] Introduction & The Problem
*(Screen shows the main ClimateGuard UI. Dark mode, looking clean.)*

**"Hello everyone! We are thrilled to present ClimateGuard for the Gemma 4 Good Hackathon.** 

**When a climate disaster strikes, the first things to go down are the power grid and the internet. In those critical minutes, people are left blind, not knowing what to do.**

**ClimateGuard is a 100% offline, AI-powered disaster preparedness assistant. It runs entirely on your local device using the incredible Google Gemma model. It requires zero internet, no cloud APIs, and zero data costs — meaning it works perfectly when you need it most."**

---

## 🕒 [0:30 - 1:15] The Solution & Smart UI
*(Hover your mouse over the disaster grid — point out the 12 disaster types.)*

**"Our interface is designed for extreme stress scenarios. We've categorized the 12 most deadly climate disasters. Let's say a flash flood is happening right now."**
*(Click the '🌊 Flood' button)*

**"Typing is hard during an emergency, so we built hands-free accessibility right in."**
*(Click the '🎙️ Voice Input' button. Speak into your microphone:)*
**"Water has entered the ground floor and is rising fast. We have two kids here."**
*(The text appears in the box automatically. Do not press enter, let the auto-submit trigger the 'GET SURVIVAL GUIDANCE' button.)*

**"The moment you stop speaking, ClimateGuard automatically submits your situation and queries the offline Gemma model."**

---

## 🕒 [1:15 - 2:00] The Gemma AI Response & TTS
*(Scroll down to show the generated Response Panel. Point out the sections.)*

**"Gemma instantly generates a highly structured, localized survival plan. It gives us Immediate Actions, Shelter advice, Supply checklists, and crucially—real Emergency Contacts for our region."**

**"For accessibility, if a user is visually impaired or injured, we integrated offline Text-to-Speech."**
*(Click the '🔊 Read Aloud' button. Let it read the first sentence, then click '⏹️ Stop Reading'.)*
**"The browser natively reads the instructions aloud, all without touching the internet."**

---

## 🕒 [2:00 - 2:30] Offline GPS Satellite Location & Map (Zero Internet Needed)
*(Scroll back up to the Location box. Click '📍 Use My Location'.)*

**"Next, we demonstrate our offline spatial intelligence. When cell towers are down, normal web geocoding APIs are dead. That is why ClimateGuard's GPS system is engineered to work 100% offline."**
*(The coordinates box populates with raw GPS coordinates e.g. "25.5941, 85.1376", showing the yellow warning: "📡 Using GPS coordinates (offline mode)")*

**"By reading direct satellite GPS hardware signals, the application parses raw latitude and longitude offline, instantly loading pre-cached map tile graphics to draw the precise hazard impact circle."**
*(The Leaflet map slides down, showing the safety boundary circle for the flood zone.)*

**"If these offline GPS coordinates map to a high-risk region like Patna, India..."**
*(Scroll down to the response panel)*
**"ClimateGuard automatically detects the locality, and our fine-tuned Gemma model dynamically renders the critical survival steps in Hindi alongside English side-by-side, ensuring rural volunteers get identical, life-saving clarity."**

---

## 🕒 [2:30 - 3:00] Export & Conclusion
*(Point to the buttons at the bottom of the response)*

**"Finally, users can export their incident report to a PDF or share it instantly with rescue workers via SMS or Bluetooth."**

**"ClimateGuard proves that with Google's Gemma models, we can bring world-class, life-saving AI to the most remote and disconnected parts of the world. Thank you!"**
